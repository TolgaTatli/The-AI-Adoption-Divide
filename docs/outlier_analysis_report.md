### 2. Potential Explanations

- **Digital Leapfrogging**: Developing countries may skip traditional tech and adopt AI directly
- **Youth Demographics**: Younger populations are more tech-savvy
- **Mobile-First**: High mobile penetration enables AI adoption
- **Education Focus**: Investment in STEM education despite lower GDP
